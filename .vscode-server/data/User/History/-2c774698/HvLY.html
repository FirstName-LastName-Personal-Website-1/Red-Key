Project Applehead

Apple head is a larger than life chihuahua that is small and so terrified of his surroundings
Ordinary things seem like terrifying objects to him for example
your everyday singing tree bird appears as a hawk to him, a remote control car seems like a monstertruck to him
a basketball becomes a boulder to him. It creates an adventure like realm for our small easily frighten dog.
Apple head is a brave dog who gets his courage from barking and from the help of his friends from nature like frank the tortoise.
Sit down with friends and family and watch the upcoming 'Apple Head' animated series coming out 2024.
