Project Applehead

Apple head is a larger than life chihuahua that is small and so terrified of his surroundings that ordinary things become monstrous obstacles to him for example your everyday singing tree bird appears as a hawk to him, a remote control car seems like a monstertruck to him a basketball becomes a giant boulder to him. It creates an adventure like realm for our small easily frighten dog.
Apple head is a brave dog who gets his courage from barking, eating table food (like spaghetti) and from the help of his friends from nature like dougley the tortoise and spaz the cat.
Sit down with friends and family and watch the upcoming 'Apple Head' animated series coming out 2024.

Project Fitavitar

Pronounced fit-tah-ve-tar, Project Fitavitar is a revolutionary way of encouraging gamers to workout by allowing them to create a video game character who's stamina, strength and superhuman senses reflects your overall weekly workout routine. For example, just clocking in no more than 3 of your weekly workouts will increase your characters overall stats and attributes.
Make them stronger, faster and in some cases superhuman. Those who log their workouts more than others will see that their avitars are not like the others. Our smart technology watch monitors your workout when activated; it is guided by motion capture that gets its reading from an invisible bubble it places around the wearer, that way when activated. Fitavitar will know how many steps you're taking, when youre jumping or doing pull-ups or even when you're lifting weights. All this information going into your character making him stronger and more. Not only that you can emmerse you character into our evolutionary meta-verse based video game where you beat levels and team up with other fitavitars around the world. Become part of the fit meta-verse when Fitavitar makes it debut fall 2025!
