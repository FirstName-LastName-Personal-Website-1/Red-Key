Project Applehead

Ordinary things seem like terrifying objects to him for example

Project Fitavitar

Pronounced fit-tah-ve-tar, Project Fitavitar is an revolutionary way of encouraging gamers to workout by allowing them to create a video game character who's stamina, strength and superhuman senses reflects your overall weekly workout routine. For example, just clocking in your weekly workouts (no more than 3 workouts a week) will give increase your characters overall attributes.
Making them stronger, faster and in some cases superhuman. Those log their workouts more than others will see that their avitars are not like the others. Our smart technology watch monitors your workout when activated; it is guided by motion capture that gets its reading an invisible it places around the wearer, that way when activated. Fitavitar will know how many steps youre taking, when youre jumping or doing pull-ups or even when youre lifting weights. All this information going into your character making him stronger and more. Not only that you can emmerse you character into our evolutionary meta-verse based video game where you beat levels and team up with other fitavitars around the world. Become part of the fit meta-verse when Fitavitar makes it debut fall 2025!
